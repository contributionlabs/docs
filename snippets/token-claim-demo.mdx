<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Intro">
    The intro page is the first page that users see when they land on your claim
    page. It's a great place to share context about why you're doing the airdrop,
    token distribution, or rewards distribution and to showcase your brand.
    ![view-1](https://megaphone.b-cdn.net/docs/demo-claim-view-1.png)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Allocation">
    On the allocation checker page, users will paste in their wallet address to
    check if they're eligible for the airdrop claim.
    ![view-2](https://megaphone.b-cdn.net/docs/demo-claim-view-2.png)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Reward">
    If the user is eligible for the airdrop, they will see their allocation
    displayed in a nice card. The criteria for eligibility and rewards breakdown can
    be configured based on the snapshot you provide to us during setup.
    ![view-3](https://megaphone.b-cdn.net/docs/demo-claim-view-3.png)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="(Optional) Promotion">
    After the user has seen their allocation and eligibility status, you can
    choose to show a call to action to promote a product activation, link to a
    website, a Twitter intent, etc.
    ![view-1](https://megaphone.b-cdn.net/docs/promo-modal.png)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Claim">
    When the user is ready to claim, they will accept the terms and conditions, and
    then execute a claim transaction.
    ![view-4](https://megaphone.b-cdn.net/docs/demo-claim-view-4a.png)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="(Optional) Video Ad">
    You can optionally show a video to your users during the transaction processing.
    This is a really fun way to keep your community engaged and excited during the
    transaction processing.
    ![view-5](https://megaphone.b-cdn.net/docs/demo-claim-view-5a.png)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Success">
    The success page is the final page that users see after they've successfully
    claimed their tokens. It's a great place to thank your users for their
    participation, share next steps, and showcase an optional commemorative NFT mint
    (powered by Megaphone).
    ![view-6](https://megaphone.b-cdn.net/docs/demo-claim-view-6.png)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
